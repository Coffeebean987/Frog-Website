Ah, where it all starts!

The life of an egg is a delicate one. You are strewn out into a vast open source of water. Well, you and your many other brothers and sisters of course! All clumped together into one small bundle of joy! This is the life of most frog eggs, where as some are laid in different areas. We'll get to that later.
