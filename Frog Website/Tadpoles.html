Wait, they morph??!

Ofcourse they morph! They go through a variety of changes before they become an adult. Just like humans, they go through changes in their body, just like us when we go through puberty. Above, you can see a tadpole just getting their first pair of legs. Their tail still remains until they grow a bit more.
